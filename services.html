Frequently Asked Questions

Guidance for your journey into the mystical arts and spiritual healing services offered by Miss Linz The Mystic.

What can I expect during my first session?

Every session begins with a brief grounding meditation to centre our energies. Whether you are receiving a Usui Reiki healing session, a tarot reading, or an aura energy reading, Miss Linz creates a safe, compassionate space. You may feel subtle warmth, tingling sensations, or a deep sense of relaxation. Afterwards, we discuss insights and practical steps for integrating the experience into your daily life.

How should I prepare energetically?

In the hours before our meeting, try to avoid heavy meals, alcohol, or excessive screen time. A quiet walk, a warm bath, or simply sitting with a cup of herbal tea can help settle the mind. Setting a simple intention—such as openness to receive guidance—allows the energies to flow more freely. Wear comfortable clothing and ensure you will not be disturbed.

Are remote and in-person sessions available?

Yes, both options are offered. Remote sessions for Reiki, tarot, and aura readings are conducted via video call, allowing us to connect across distance without diminishing the potency of the work. In-person sessions are held in a tranquil, private studio space. When you inquire, please specify your preference so we can arrange the most suitable format.

Can ceremonies be personalised?

Absolutely. As a pagan wedding officiant, Miss Linz collaborates closely with each couple to craft a ceremony that reflects your unique spiritual path—whether that includes handfasting, elemental blessings, ancestral acknowledgements, or seasonal symbolism. Every ritual is co-created to honour your love story authentically.

Is this work inclusive of all spiritual paths?

Yes, deeply so. Miss Linz The Mystic welcomes seekers from every background, tradition, and belief system. The services draw from witchcraft, pagan traditions, and energy healing, but they are offered as tools for empowerment and insight—never as dogma. You are invited to bring your full self, exactly as you are.

How do I inquire about availability?

The easiest way is through the contact form on this site. Share a little about the service you are drawn to and your preferred days or times. Miss Linz personally responds to every inquiry within two business days. For those seeking a psychic medium session or past life guided walk back, a brief introductory call may be arranged beforehand.
